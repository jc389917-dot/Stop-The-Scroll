What is Harassment?

Harassment is repeated unwanted behaviour that makes a person feel unsafe, targeted, or uncomfortable. It can happen online or offline.

Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying is harassment that happens through digital platforms such as social media, messaging apps, or games.

Common Examples

  • Sending abusive or threatening messages
  • Spreading rumours or false information
  • Sharing embarrassing content without permission
  • Repeated targeting in games or chats

Warning Signs

Avoiding devices, sudden anxiety after being online, or withdrawing from social media may indicate harassment.

Why It Matters

Online harassment can seriously affect mental health, confidence, and wellbeing. Even small actions online can have a real impact.
